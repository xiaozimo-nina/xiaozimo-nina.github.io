  • 3 POINTS

    7.1 Analysis

    1. Methods of researching an existing system;

    2. Recording and analysing information about the current system;

    3. System specification.

  • 6 POINTS

    7.2 Design

    1. Describe how it is necessary to design documents;

    2. Be able to produce designs to solve a given problem;

    3. Design data capture forms and screen layouts;

    4. Design report layouts and screen displays;

    5. Design validation routines;

    6. Design the required data/file structures.

  • 3 POINTS

    7.3 Development and testing

    1. Testing designs;

    2. Testing strategies;

    3. Improvements needed as a result of testing.

  • 2 POINTS

    7.4 Implementation

    1. Different methods of system implementation;

    2. Methods of implementation.

  • 2 POINTS

    7.5 Documentation

    1. Technical documentation for an information system;

    2. User documentation for an information system.

  • 2 POINTS

    7.6 Evaluation

    1. Describe the need to evaluate a solution in terms of the efficiency of the solution, the ease of use of the solution, and the appropriateness of the solution;

    2. Describe the need for a variety of evaluation strategies.

  • 3 POINTS

    8.1 Physical safety

    1. Describe common physical safety issues and what causes them;

    2. Describe some simple strategies for preventing these issues;

    3. Evaluate own use of IT equipment and develop strategies to minimise the potential safety risks.

  • 8 POINTS

    8.2 E-Safety

    1. Explain what is meant by personal data;

    2. Explain why personal data should be confidential and protected;

    3. Explain how to avoid inappropriate disclosure of personal data;

    4. Discuss why eSafety is needed;

    5. Evaluate own use of the internet and use strategies to minimise the potential dangers;

    6. Evaluate own use of email and use strategies to minimise the potential dangers;

    7. Evaluate own use of social media/networking sites, instant messaging and internet chat rooms and use strategies to minimise the potential dangers;

    8. Describe measures which should be taken when playing games on the internet.

  • 2 POINTS

    8.3 Security of data

    1. Effective security of data;

    2. Security of data online.
